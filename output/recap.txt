🌍 MARKET NEWS RECAP

Global markets traded mixed today as the early-year rally cooled, with the S&P 500 index falling 0.3% while the Nasdaq composite managed a modest gain of 0.2%. 💼 PORTFOLIO FOCUS

The technology sector saw a notable rotation today, with Alphabet (GOOGL) shares surging approximately 2.5% on optimism surrounding its AI-driven growth, briefly pushing its market value past Apple (AAPL). Apple (AAPL) extended its slide, retreating further from its recent high as investors weighed surging component costs and a shift out of high-valuation growth names. Chipmaker Nvidia (NVDA) remained resilient, gaining around 1.0% and benefiting from its position as a primary supplier in the face of ongoing memory shortages. Microsoft (MSFT) dipped as some investors began demanding more concrete returns on its massive artificial intelligence capital expenditures. Tesla (TSLA) traded with elevated volatility as the electric vehicle sector continued to be impacted by the broader rotation away from high-momentum growth stocks.
