🌍 MARKET NEWS RECAP

**1. 🌍 MARKET OVERVIEW**

European markets led the global rally, with the Euro Stoxx 50 index gaining over 1.0% to hit a new record high, driven by strength in the technology and industrial sectors. In Asia, the Shanghai Composite index surged 1.1% to cross the key 4,000-point level for the first time in three months, led by a strong rally in semiconductor shares. US stocks traded mixed after the open, with the S&P 500 posting a modest gain of 0.19% while the Nasdaq Composite was largely flat, as investors digested a softer-than-expected ISM Manufacturing PMI reading of 47.9.

**2. 💼 PORTFOLIO FOCUS**

The semiconductor and Artificial Intelligence (AI) theme remains robust, with UBS reiterating a "Buy" rating on NVDA shares ahead of a scheduled keynote address. The broader tech sector buoyancy, especially in AI-related infrastructure, helped support the overall trend in the S&P 500 and Nasdaq 100, which continues to benefit MSFT and AMZN as major cloud and enterprise service providers. While no direct news was available for TSLA, the energy sector saw a lift, with related oil infrastructure stocks rallying on geopolitical developments, a trend which could indirectly affect the EV sector's input costs. Outside of Big Tech, Coinbase (COIN) received an analyst upgrade from Goldman Sachs to a "Buy," citing an attractive valuation following recent underperformance.
